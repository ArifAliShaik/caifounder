As an entrepreneur, the list of frustrations can last a lifetime. To name a few...

  • Information management

    As a startup grows, information becomes out of date quickly.

  • Company pivot management

    As a startup validates assumptions, pivots and iterations are required.

  • Articulating company value

    Entrepreneurs excel in selling product value and lack selling company value.

  • Completing contradictory activities

    Dealing with multiple mentors and advisors often results in time wasted completing contradictory activities.

  • Investor
    denials

    Approaching investors too early, lack of investor portfolio due diligence, lack of data as proof of investment readiness level.

  • Competing for attention of best advisors

    Limited personalized attention from mentors and advisors in the idea stage.

Here is the fundamental problem we solve

  • Startup ecosystem stakeholder incentive misalignment

    Although startup ecosystems are created to support entrepreneurial development, the inherent incentives of the stakeholders do not create an environment of equal support to entrepreneurs, nor the best advice in all cases.

    The Frustration

    Early stage ventures are high risk, high impact endeavours which are yet to be associated with a satisfactory solution to the unacceptable failure rate.

  • Caifounder achieves startup ecosystem stakeholder goal with entrepreneurs best interest in mind

    Natural Language Business Model Processing and Design at the fingertips of Entrepreneurs through a smartphone based on information collected, Entrepreneurs receive actionable feedback through conversational interaction on what additional information should be collected. Resulting in uniquely catered advice to entrepreneurs on their journey from Entrepreneur to Founder-CEO and from idea, to sustainable, profitable, business model, to company exit.

    Benefit Realization. Expectation Management

    Caifounder ia a diligence and risk mitigation technology for early stage high risk ventures. Our mission is to organise business model metrics and make the information universally accessible and actionable.

  • RESULT

    Progress becomes more visible, guided, and personalized. An automated interpretation of startup metrics is attained. And a streamlined referral system of corporate and startup match becomes more feasible.
