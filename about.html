CAIFOUDNER's Mission

Caifounder's mission is to organize business model metrics and make the information universally accessible and actionable.

HERE IS THE FUNDAMENTAL PROBLEM WE SOLVE

Startup ecosystem stakeholder incentive misalignment - Although startup ecosystems are created to support entrepreneurial development, the inherent incentives of the stakeholders do not create an environment of equal support to entrepreneurs, nor the best advice in all cases.

Caifounder achieves startup ecosystem stakeholder goal with entrepreneurs best interest in mind - Natural Language Business Model Processing and Design at the fingertips of Entrepreneurs through a smartphone - Based on information collected, Entrepreneurs receive actionable feedback through conversational interaction on what additional information should be collected. - Resulting in uniquely catered advice to entrepreneurs on their journey from Entrepreneur to Founder-CEO and from idea, to sustainable, profitable, business model, to company exit.

RESULT

Progress becomes more visible, guided, and personalized. An automated interpretation of startup metrics is attained. And a streamlined referral system of corporate and startup match becomes more feasible.
